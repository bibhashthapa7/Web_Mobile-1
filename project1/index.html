Set against the backdrop of the Himalayas, the mountainous country of Nepal is famous for its majestic landscapes, cordial people, incredible hospitality and eclectic cuisine influenced by the countryâ€™s ethnic diversity. Sharing a traditionally prepared homemade meal with the guests is a true honor for the people of Nepal. What makes this food so unique is the combination of creativity, elegance and simplicity of the cooking style and subtle use of many spices and herbs native to Nepal and India, such as cumin, coriander, garlic and mustard.

Nepali Kitchen is a local and authentic restaurant located at the heart of Rochester, in Henrietta, aiming to uplift and promote the unique Nepali food culture. We offer you diverse Nepali and continental cuisine with traditional recipes and the perfect dining experience.

The restaurant is open daily all week from 02:00pm till 10:00pm.
